Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Visualizations

There is a not a strong, descernable pattern in the chart above which plots latitude and cloudiness. There is a concentration of citites with 0% cloudiness that spans all latitudes. Additionally, there are several other bands of cities with cloudiness at 75% and 95%. While there is no trend that shows a change in cloudiness based on latitude, it is interesting to see the clusters of cloudiness at certain cloudiness percentages.
